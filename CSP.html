Computer Science Principles or CSP, is a course that is designed to introduce students to Computer Science. This course is an introductory course. This course is an AP for Sophomores, Juniors, and Seniors. This course is really fundemental for students interested in majoring Computer Science. To add on, CSP introduces students to visual based programming in the beginning, and we make our way up to text-based coding. CSP is a PLTW course if you didn't know already. Meaning this course is designed to help student participation in a certain subject/topic.
