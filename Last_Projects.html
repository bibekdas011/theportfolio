PLTW 2.2.1 Stuxnet Report

Partners: Atharva Tawde, Michael Ng

Start: 11/10/2020 Deadline: 11/13/2020

In this project, we had to research different documents regarding the Stuxnet attacks, select a debate position on whether or not it was justified, and create a slide representing our position.Our creative process: began as us discussing whether or not it was actually justified in a conversation format. Afterwards, we put our ideas into a list and then converted them into a Google Slide. The pertinent questions we were asked were: "Was Stuxnet Justified?" and "Should Cyber Warfare be internationally banned similar to chemical warfare". The position we took on was: Pro, Cyberware shouldn't banned and Stuxnet was justifiable

Interactive Seating Chart

Peers: Whole Class

Start: 09/06/2021 Deadline: 09/15/2021

We had to implement one of the abstract classes and demonstrated mastery of Github worflow as the whole class pushed to a single repository compiling into one final file.
